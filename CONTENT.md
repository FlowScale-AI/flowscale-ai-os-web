# The Problem

## AI Workflows Are Powerful — But Not Production Ready

Creative teams experimenting with generative AI encounter three major barriers.

### Fragmented Tooling

Studios rely on dozens of disconnected AI tools, each with different interfaces, subscriptions, and limitations.

### Creative IP at Risk

Most AI tools operate on cloud infrastructure, forcing studios to send sensitive assets and client work outside their production environment.

### Workflows Built for Engineers

Local AI workflows exist, but they are complex node graphs designed for technical users rather than artists.

---

# The Solution

## Turn AI Workflows Into Production Infrastructure

FlowScale bridges the gap between experimental AI workflows and real production systems.

Technical teams build workflows using open tools such as ComfyUI or custom pipelines.

FlowScale converts those workflows into production‑ready applications.

These applications run locally across studio infrastructure, allowing artists to use powerful AI tools without interacting with node graphs or code.
